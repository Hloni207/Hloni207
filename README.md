### 📖 My Journey

The Spark: My journey began in 2025 during my first week of college. What started as a classroom requirement quickly turned into an obsession. I began with Python scripts, fell in love with the logic, and am now diving deep into the complexities of C++.

The Workflow: I am a "Vibe Coder" at heart, but my process is highly strategic. I leverage a multi-model AI workflow—utilizing Claude, Qwen, Gemini, and Zed—to accelerate ideation and frontend design, allowing me to ship high-quality projects faster.

The Hardware Connection: When I’m not in the IDE, you’ll find me in the world of Electronics and Robotics. From breadboarding temporary prototypes to testing systems at robotics firms, I believe the future of tech lies in the seamless integration of systems and physical machines.
